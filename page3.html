Crunches

Crunches are a great exercise for strengthening the abdominal muscles.

Benefits of Crunches

  • Strengthens the core muscles
  • Improves posture and balance
  • Can be modified to suit different fitness levels

How to Perform a Proper Crunch

  1. Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor.
  2. Place your hands behind your head or cross them over your chest.
  3. Engage your core and lift your upper body towards your knees.
  4. Lower your upper body back to the starting position with control.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
