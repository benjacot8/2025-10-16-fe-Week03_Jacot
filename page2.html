Pull-Ups

Pull-ups are an excellent exercise for strengthening the back and biceps.

Benefits of Pull-Ups

  • Strengthens the upper back, shoulders, and biceps
  • Improves grip strength
  • Can be modified to suit different fitness levels

How to Perform a Proper Pull-Up

  1. Grab the pull-up bar with your palms facing away from you and hands shoulder-width apart.
  2. Hang with your arms fully extended.
  3. Pull yourself up until your chin is above the bar.
  4. Lower yourself back to the starting position with control.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
